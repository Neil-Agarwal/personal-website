Education

University of Waterloo

Bachelor of Applied Science, Systems Design Engineering
I have always been interested in solving complex problems. However, more times than not, complex problems tend to require an inter-disciplinary approach where one needs to understand all the aspects of a system to truly craft a beautiful solution. This is precisely why I chose to study Systems Design Engineering. It is a hollistic approach to engineering where students learn to understand all aspects of engineering. Using this knowledge and a big picture approach, they can then solve complex problems which require an understanding of software, electrical, computer, chemical, and management engineering. With a focus on design methodlogies, Systems Design Engineers are incredible at identifying solutions that not only tackle the problem at hand but are a joy to use. By existing at the intersection of technological ingenuity and design, there is no problem that Systems Design Engineers are not equipped to solve.
